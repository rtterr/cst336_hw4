About

In today's technology-reliant world, information security has become more important than ever in order to protect the computer systems that house and transport our precious data.

There are three primary concepts that summarize how data can be made more (or less) secure: confidentiality, integrity, and availability. Systems that fulfill all three concepts can be said to be comprehensively secure.
